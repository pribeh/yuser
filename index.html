Introducing yuser

Are you sick and tired of not being able to monetize your social media presence or feel rewarded for your efforts?

Yuser is being constructed from the ground up to reward you with points and cryptocurrency for your content, interaction, and status. Yuser is THE solution you’ve been waiting for.

The Challenge

Influencers struggle to feel rewarded for their efforts and status on social media. They have seen their reach decline over the past few years across various major platforms. They’ve also seen a dip in earnings from monetizable platforms.

You're an Influencer. A Trendsetter. A Creator. A Visionary. You can see what others can’t. The Internet of value is the future. Be a part of it. Be first. Grow your online presence, unlock new features and earn cryptocurrency.

The Solution ❤️ 💯

We recognize that tastemakers and creators are the driving force of any social platform, that’s why we’ve set out to create a social network that properly rewards you. It’s in the name, yuser is built for users. You create your own content and we won’t control how much of it your audience can see. Earn points that let you unlock cool features and digital assets or exchange them for cryptocurrency.
